You have a shop and within a shop there are doors to the entrance.
Door can be automatically open, they can be armored, 
they can be with alarm, or plain.

if doors are with alarm, they can be enabled or disabled.
If alarm is enabled, then when doors open, it will start a timer.
If timer times out, it will trigger the alarm.
Timer stop as soon as the doors closes

Triggered alarm notifies SecurityCenter. They will rush to the shop.
